Notes and abbreviations for 1841 Census:
Ages are estimated and rounded down to the nearest 5 years if not known.
M.S. = Male Servant
F.S. = Female Servant
J =  Journeyman
Ap = Apprentice
Sh = Shopman
M = Manufr.
m = Maker
S = Scotland
I = Ireland
F = Foreign

Note: Spellings were very haphazard, particularly 'Leaber' or 'Leabor',
and it looks as if someone has later interpreted them as 'Labourer'.
