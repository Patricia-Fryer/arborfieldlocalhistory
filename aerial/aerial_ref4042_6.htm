The 'Starfish' decoy site during WWII

Modern aerial photo of the 'Starfish' site, courtesy of Multimaps

RAF aerial photo of Lower Arborfield and Shinfield, dated 10th March 1946, ref. 4042, reproduced by permission of the National Monuments Record, English Heritage.

The top-secret 'Starfish' decoy site at Arborfield Hall Farm was dismantled very promptly once it was no longer deemed necessary, and by March 1946 the area had been almost completely cleared, as can be seen at the top right of this photo. However, the adjacent meadow just to the south had seen a lot of recent activity.
