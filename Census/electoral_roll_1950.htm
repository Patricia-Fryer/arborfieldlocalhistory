Many years' worth of Electoral Registers are held by Berkshire Record Office on microfiche.
Spellings have been preserved in this transcript.

This Electoral Register shows the combined parish of Arborfield and Newland, and records the large number of families making their temporary home in the grounds of Arborfield Hall. At least 84 electors lived in and around the Hall.

More research needs to be done on the dwellings simply named 'Council Houses'. Most of these would have received new house numbers when the Garrison Bypass was built, because they are in what are now Rickman Close and Bramshill Close.
