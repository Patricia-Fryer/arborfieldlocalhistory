Arborfield Local History Society has met several times since 2003.

Over several months in early 2003, we prepared for our History Exhibition which was held at the end of May at the Junior School.  The main hall was completely filled with display-boards.

On November 10th, Berkshire Record Office revealed its secrets, from the many maps of Arborfield to early registers and documents of the parish.

On December 8th, we visited the mansion at Sindlesham, once home of John Walter, the proprietor of 'The Times'. After housing Canadian forces during World War 1, it became the Royal Merchant Navy School and later Bearwood College, whose Archivist explained its history and showed us around.
