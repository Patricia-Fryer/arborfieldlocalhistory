Empire Day in 1926

 

 

Here is a well-worn photo of Empire Day at Arborfield, some time in the late 1930's. Point and click on part of the picture for a close-up view. We've identified several of the children, as shown in the close-ups.

Can you name any of the children, or correct our identification? If so, please let us know.
