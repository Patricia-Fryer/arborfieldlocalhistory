The Sunday Service was led by Rev. David Rowe and accompanied by a large Church orchestra. People assembled at the British Legion and walked along Link Way to the School Field for the Service. David was well-known for his gift of evangelism, and he featured his famous story-board during the service. A set of rectangles gradually changed to a set of phrases, which took some people longer than others to guess.
