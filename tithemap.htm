There are known to be four copies of the 1839 Tithe Apportionment Map; one for the Church (kept in the Junior School), one for the civil Parish (held by the Local History Society), one at the Berkshire Record Office, and one to the Tithe Commissioner in London.

The Map shows all land holdings within the old Arborfield Parish, and excludes any land in the Liberty of Newland, so there is a curious gap where Arborfield Cross was, excluding the 'Bull', the Parish Cottages and other buildings further along the Swallowfield Road.
