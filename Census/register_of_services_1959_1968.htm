The St. Bartholomew's Arborfield Register of Services, December 1959 - September 1968 not only listed the Sunday services and the numbers attending, but it also listed Baptisms, Marriages, Funerals and other special services, at least up to 1963.

Note: This register covered the incumbencies of C Hamilton Wilson and Geoffrey Carr.
Both of them recorded cases of extreme weather, which have been listed here. However, Geoffrey didn't record names of baptisms, nor the occurrence of weddings and funerals.
