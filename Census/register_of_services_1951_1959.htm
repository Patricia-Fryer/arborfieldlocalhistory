The St. Bartholomew's Arborfield Register of Services, February 1951 - November 1959 not only listed the Sunday services and the numbers attending, but it also listed Baptisms, Marriages, Funerals and other special services.

Note: This register covered the incumbencies of Claude Sparshott and C Hamilton Wilson.
Both of them recorded cases of extreme weather, which have been listed here.
