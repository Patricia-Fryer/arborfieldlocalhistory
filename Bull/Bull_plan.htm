Refused Planning Application, 2006

The planning application 7378, and Listed Building Consent, 7379, were refused in 2006. The Grade II Listed building was described as follows:

From the front, the 'Bull' would have hardly changed, but it was a different story behind the frontage:

Side Elevation from Eversley Road

The restaurant would have been more than twice as deep as the original building, though the toilet block would have been demolished..

Rear Elevation from Car Park

Two similar barn-type extensions and a conservatory would have greeted customers arriving in the car park:

Plan of Restaurant

The floor plan shows that the original area would almost have trebled, with a very large entrance lobby opening onto Eversley Road.
